Associated Press

Known for being the standard in objective reporting, its no surprise the AP has such a robust website. Navigation links are descriptive, comprehensive in quantity, and any donation links are out of the way of content and clearly marked as such.

Steam

The near-only option for online PC gaming, there’s a reason why the Steam desktop client simply loads the store website page in a container instead of having its own UI. Breadcrumbs on any given game’s page allow you to quickly backtrack to a page with similar games, and the search feature gives you thumbnails of suggestions, so you don’t have to click through each one if you have an idea of the game’s name and its cover art.
